# Getting started on Fit Finder — Trainer guide

Everything you need to go from sign-up to your first client message. The whole process takes about 10 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create your account (1 min)

- Go to [fitfinder.co](https://fitfinder.co) and click **Get Started**
- Enter your name, email, and a password (8+ characters)
- You'll be taken straight into the onboarding flow

## Step 2: Choose your role (30 sec)

- Select **Trainer** (or **Both** if you also want to browse trainers yourself)
- Click Next

## Step 3: Complete your profile (3 min)

- **Name:** your real name as clients will see it
- **Bio:** 2-3 sentences about your background and coaching style. Be specific — "I specialise in strength training for busy professionals" beats "I'm passionate about fitness"
- **City and country:** where you're based (clients filter by location)
- **Languages:** which languages you coach in
- **Coaching mode:** Online, In-Person, or Hybrid
- **Profile photo:** a clear headshot or training photo (not a gym selfie)

## Step 4: Add your trainer details (2 min)

- **Specialties:** pick all that apply (Strength Training, Fat Loss, HIIT, Yoga, Rehab & Mobility, etc.)
- **Years of experience**
- **Certifications:** list your qualifications (e.g., "NASM CPT", "Level 3 PT", "NSCA CSCS")
- **Price range:** your minimum and maximum per session or package in your local currency
- **Availability notes:** e.g., "Evenings and weekends" or "Flexible schedule — message me"

## Step 5: Connect Stripe to get paid (2 min)

- Go to your **Dashboard**
- Click **Connect Stripe** in the payment card
- You'll be redirected to Stripe to create or connect your account
- Once connected, you'll see a green **Connected** badge on your dashboard
- Fit Finder takes a 12.8% fee on each transaction — the rest goes directly to your Stripe account

## Step 6: Create your first plan (1 min)

- From your Dashboard, click **Create Plan**
- Add a title (e.g., "4-Week Strength Programme"), description, and price
- Choose one-time or monthly billing
- Your plan will appear on your public profile for clients to purchase

## Step 7: Share your profile

- Your profile is live at `fitfinder.co/profile/[your-id]`
- Share this link on your social media, in your Instagram bio, or with existing clients
- When someone messages you, you'll see it in your inbox on the platform

---

## Tips for a great profile

- **Complete every field** — profiles with 100% completion appear higher in search results
- **Your bio is your elevator pitch:** specific beats generic every time
- **Upload a real photo** — profiles with photos get significantly more messages
- **Set a competitive price range** — clients filter by budget, so make sure you're visible
- **Update your availability notes** regularly so clients know when you're free
