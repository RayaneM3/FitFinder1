# Fit Finder

### A new way for clients to find you

We're inviting select personal trainers to join our platform.

---

## The problem

Clients want to find a trainer but hate the gym sales pitch. They scroll Instagram, ask around, or just give up. Meanwhile, trainers spend hours on content marketing and chasing leads instead of actually coaching.

## What Fit Finder is

Fit Finder is a marketplace where clients discover trainers by specialty, location, and coaching style — then reach out when they're ready. No cold outreach. No bidding wars. Clients initiate every conversation, so every lead that messages you is already interested. Think of it as a shopfront for your coaching business that works while you're training.

## How it works for trainers

1. **Create your profile** — specialties, certifications, pricing, availability, coaching mode (online / in-person / hybrid)
2. **Clients find you** through search and filters — your profile is your shopfront
3. **They message you directly** when they're interested — you reply and build the relationship
4. **They purchase your plan** through the platform — you get paid via Stripe, minus a 12.8% platform fee

## What makes it different

- **Privacy-first:** client locations shown at city level only, trainers can't cold-message — every conversation starts because a client chose you
- **No subscription fee:** you only pay the 12.8% fee when you actually get paid — zero cost until you earn
- **You own the relationship:** once a client purchases, you work with them directly on your terms
